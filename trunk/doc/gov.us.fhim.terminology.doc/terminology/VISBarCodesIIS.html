VIS Bar Codes (IIS)

Value Set VIS Bar Codes (IIS) - 2.16.840.1.114222.4.11.6041
Code System CDC GS1 VIS BarCodes - 2.16.840.1.114222.4.5.307
Version 9
Definition The purpose of the barcode on the bottom of the Vaccine Information Statement (VIS) is to provide an opportunity to electronically capture the VIS document type (e.g. influenza, MMR) and the edition date of the VIS, as required by the National Childhood Vaccine Injury Act (NCVIA). For more information, please visit - http://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pubs/vis/vis-barcodes.htm
