Evidence Of Immunity (PHVS_IIS)

Value Set Evidence Of Immunity (PHVS_IIS) - 2.16.840.1.114222.4.11.3293
Version 1
Source SNOMED-CT
Source URL http://phinvads.cdc.gov/vads/ViewValueSet.action?id=FEE9E077-ADB9-DF11-9BDD-0015173D1785
Definition Evidence of immunity indicates that a person has plausible evidence that they have already developed immunity to a particular disease. The definition of plausible evidence is a local decision, but best practice would suggest that serological evidence of immunity is the strongest indicator of immunity.
